Tumblr URL: What's this?
The Tumblr URL is just that: the URL of your Tumblr. Go to your Tumblr, copy and paste the URL into that box up there, and save that bad boy. It'll often look something like

http://myblog.tumblr.com

If you directed it to another domain, it might look something like

http://blog.dwen.me

</shameless plug>. Twumblrface needs this to be able to post on Tumblr.
Facebook Username: What's this?
Your Facebook username is what comes after 'www.facebook.com/' when you go to your profile page. For example, if your Facebook profile's URL is

http://www.facebook.com/foobar

then you'd want to put 'foobar' in the box above and hit save. Twumblrface needs this to be able to post status updates on Facebook.
